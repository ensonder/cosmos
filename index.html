TRINE
ASPECT
Planets 120° apart. The trine is the most harmonious aspect — energy flows easily, naturally, and supportively between the two planets. It represents innate talent, flow, and effortless cooperation. Often so easy it goes unnoticed.
Your natal: ☉ Sun in Libra trines ☽ Moon in Gemini — your core identity and emotional nature flow together naturally.

SQUARE
ASPECT
Planets 90° apart. The square creates tension, friction, and dynamic challenge between two planetary energies. Squares force action, growth, and resolution — they are the engine of achievement when worked with consciously. Without awareness, they create recurring conflicts.

OPPOSITION
ASPECT
Planets 180° apart — directly across the zodiac wheel from each other. The opposition creates a tug-of-war between two energies that must be integrated rather than split. Often projected onto relationships — one person embodies one side, another the other.
